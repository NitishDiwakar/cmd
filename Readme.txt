ABOUT THESE NOTES
=================

This website contains my personal study notes, organized by subjects and topics.  
Use the sidebar on the left to open folders and choose a file to read. On mobile devices, the notes may appear below the menu after you select a file, so simply scroll down to start reading.

Some longer pages include quick jump links that let you move directly to a section or return to the top of the page. If a line shows a small camera icon, it means a diagram or image is available — clicking it will open the image in a new tab.

Everything here is kept minimal on purpose: plain text, fast loading, and no distractions. The goal is to make revision quick and comfortable, whether you are reading a few lines or exploring an entire topic.
