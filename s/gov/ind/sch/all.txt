Agneepath Yojana

- Scheme Announcement - June 14,2022.
- Qualification -10th-12th pass (Age -17.5 to 23 years).
- Under this, youth will be recruited in all the three services (land, water, air) for 4 years, which will be called ‘Agniveer’.
- On completion of four years. Agniveers will be paid Rs 11.71 lakh under the service fund package. On completion of service, Agniveers will be given 10% reservation in CAPF and Assam Rifles. Also 25% of Agniveers will be selected permanently on the basis of merit.
- 46000 thousand Agniveers will be recruited in the first year.

AMRUT Yojana

- Launched - June 24, 2015
- AMRUT : Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and UrbanTransformation

Atal Pension Yojana

- Launched - 9th May, 2015 by Ministry of Finance.
- Under this scheme, any person in the age group of 18 to 40 years can get a pension of 1 thousand to 5 thousand on the basis of the amount deposited by him in the bank.

Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao Yojana

- Launched - 22nd January, 2015 by Ministry of Women and Child Development
- Objective - To promote gender equality and increase sex ratio

Deendayal Upadhyaya Gram Jyoti Yojana

- Launched - 25th July, 2015.
- Objective - To electrify all the villages, improve the financial condition of the distribution companies and do proper monitoring

Deen Dayal Upadhyay 'SPARSH' Yojana

- Launched - November 3, 2017
- SPARSH : Scholarship for Promotion of Aptitude and Research in Stamp as a Hobby.
- It is a scholarship scheme with the aim of increasing interest in postage stamp collection among school children of class 6th to 9th.
- Benefits - Max. 40 students get total scholarship of Rs.6000/ year.

DIKSHA Portal

- Launched -5th September, 2017 by Ministry of Human Resource Development
- This portal is helpful in training and empowering the teacher.

Digital India Mission

- Launched - 1st July, 2015
- Objective - To make all government services available to the public electronically

Make in India

- Launched - 25 September, 2014
- Purpose - Promotion of manufacturing in the country

Mission Indradhanush

- Launched - 25th December, 2014 by Union Health Ministry
- There will be vaccination for 7 diseases (Diphtheria, Whooping cough, Tetanus, Polio, TB, Measles and Hepatitis 'B') under this.

Namami Gange Scheme

- Launched - 10th July, 2014 by Ministry of Water Resources
- Purpose - For the protection and purification of river Ganga

PM Janman Yojana

- Launch - November 15, 2023 (by PM Modi, from Jharkhand)
- PM Janjati Adivasi Nyaya Maha Abhiyan (PM Janman) aims to improve the socio-economic conditions of particularly vulnerable tribal groups.
- This scheme focuses on 11 important tasks through 9 ministries. (Duration : 2023-24 to 2025-26)
- in this campaign with a total outlay of Rs. 24,104 crore, the share of the Center and the states will be Rs. 15,336 crore and Rs. 8,768 crore respectively.

PM Surya Ghar : Muft Bijli Yojana

- Launch - February 13, 2024 (by PM Modi)
- It is also known as PM Suryodaya Yojana.
- Under this, solar panels will be installed on the rooftops of 1 crore houses, from which up to 300 units of free electricity will be availabve every month.
- Under this, there is also a provison of subsidy up to Rs. 78000.

PM Vishwakarma Yojana

- Launch - September 17, 2023 (from New Delhi).
- Objective - To provide loans at minimum interest rate without guarantee to 30 lakh families of traditional craftsmen and artisans.
- A budget provision of Rs. 13000 crore has been made for this,
- Under this, 18 traditional businesses have been included.

PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi Scheme

- Launched - February 1- 2019 by Piyush Goyal.
- Purpose - Rs.6000 direct income support to all the farmers.
- This assistance will be transferred directly to the account of farmers in three equal installments of Rs.2000 each.

PM Awas Yojana

- Launched - June 25, 2015
- Objective - To build 2 crore new houses by 2022

PM Jan Aarogya Yojana - Ayushman Bharat

- Launched - 23rd September, 2018 from Ranchi (Jharkhand).
- Objective - To provide free cashless health facility up to 5 lac.

PM Jan-Dhan Yojana

- Launched - August 28, 2014 by Ministry of Finance
- Purpose - At least one bank account in all households
- Benefits - Accident insurance of Rs. 2 lakh

PM Krishi Sinchayee Yojana

- Launched - 1st July, 2015.
- Purpose - To provide water to farmers for agriculture

PM Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana - Saubhagya

- Launched - 25th September, 2017 by Ministry of Power.

PM Vaya Vandana Yojana

- Launch - July 21, 2017
- Purpose - Guaranteed return of 8% for 10 years to senior citizens in the age of 60 or more on opting for monthly pension option.

Prime Minister 'MUDRA' Scheme

- Launched - April 8, 2015.
- MUDRA : Micro Units Development & Refinance Agency.
- Purpose : To provide financial assistance and to provide loans (Types - Shishu, Kishor and Tarun) for micro enterprises sector.
- There is a provision to give loans up to Rs. 50 thousand under Shishu, Rs. 5 lakh under Kishor and Rs. 10 lakh under Tarun.

Prime Minister Kisan Maandhan Yojana

- Launched - September 12, 2019 at Ranchi (Jharkhand).
- Benefit - Minimum Rs.3000 per month pension on attaining the age of 60 years.
- Eligible for the scheme - Farmers of 18 to 40 years who have agricultural land up to 2 hectares only.

Prime Minister Shram Yogi Maandhan Yojana

- Launched - February 15, 2019.
- Eligible for the scheme - Unorganized sector workers in the age. group of 18-40 years whose monthly income is Rs 15000 or less.
- Benefit - Monthly pension of Rs.3000 on attaining the age of 60.

PM Ujjwala Yojana

Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Rurban Mission

Stand up India

- Launched - 5th April, 2016 by Ministry of Finance

Varistha Pension Bima Yojana

- Launched : January 1, 2017 by Life Corporation of India (LIC)
- Purpose - Guaranteed interest rate for 10 years to senior citizens aged 60 years and above will be 8%.
