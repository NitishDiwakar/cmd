Attorney General

Election Commision
  - Art. 324
  - Term: 6 year or 65 year
  - Qualification: not mentioned in constitution


UPSC
  - Art. 315-323


*CAG*
  - CAG, under Article 148 of the Indian Constitution, is the watchdog
      of public finance, ensuring transparency and accountability in
      government expenditure
  Detail / Power Function:
  - Auditor of Union and states
  Appt method:
    - App by president
    - *6 years or till 65 years* of age
    - Removal via impeachment


GST
  - 279A


FC
  - The Finance Commission is a constitutional body under *Article 280*,
  - established to recommend fiscal arrangements between the Union
      and States for a fixed period
  Appt method:
    - President appoints
    - A chairman and four members
    - 5 year term
