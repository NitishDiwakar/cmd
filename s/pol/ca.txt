Income-tax Act, 2025
  - Replaces the 1961
  - effective from April 1, 2026

*VB-G RAM G Act, 2025*
  - Viksit Bharat – Guarantee for Rozgar and Ajeevika Mission (Gramin)
  - Replaces the MGNREGA (2005)
  - increases 100 to *125 days* per year

Sabka Bima Sabki Raksha (Amendment of Insurance Laws) Act, 2025
  - Amends the Insurance Act, 1938 
  - 100% FDI

Banking Laws (Amendment) Act, 2025

Promotion and Regulation of Online Gaming Act, 2025
  - bans online money-based games

*SHANTI Act, 2025*
  - Sustainable Harnessing and Advancement of Nuclear Energy for Transforming India 
  - Replaces the Atomic Energy Act, 1962
  - allows regulated private sector participation 

Indian Ports Act, 2025
  - Replaces 1908 Act 

Waqf (Amendment) Act, 2025
  - UMEED Act, 1995

National Sports Governance Act, 2025
  - Establishes a binding framework for sports bodies
  - Sets up a *National Sports Tribunal*

Immigration and Foreigners Act, 2025
  - Consolidates multiple colonial-era laws like the Foreigners Act, 1946 
  - modernize immigration enforcement
  - grant statutory backing to the Bureau of Immigration

Merchant Shipping Act, 2025:
  - Replaces the 1958 Act

Protection of Interests in Aircraft Objects Act, 2025
  - Implements the *Cape Town Convention*
