*18th India State of Forest Report 2023*
- Forest Survey of India biennial assessment
- Released Dec 2024

Forest and Tree Cover
- Total forest and tree cover *8,27,356.95* sq km
- *25.17* percent of geographical area
- Forest cover *7,15,342.61* sq km
	- *21.76* percent of geographical area
- Tree cover *1,12,014.34* sq km
	- *3.41* percent of geographical area

Cover Classification Details
- Scrub area 43,622.64 sq km
- Non-forest area 24,16,489.29 sq km
- Total geographical area 32,87,468.88 sq km

Increase in Forest and Tree Cover
- Increase since last assessment *1,445.81* sq km
- Increase in forest cover *156.41* sq km
- Largest increase in combined cover — Chhattisgarh 684 sq km
- Also increase in combined: Uttar Pradesh 559 sq km; Odisha 559 sq km; Rajasthan 394 sq km
- Largest forest cover increases: Mizoram 242 sq km; Gujarat 180 sq km; Odisha 152 sq km

Largest Decrease in Forest Cover
- Madhya Pradesh 612.41 sq km
- Karnataka 459.36 sq km
- Ladakh 159.26 sq km
- Nagaland 125.22 sq km

Top States by Forest Area
- Madhya Pradesh 77,073 sq km
- Arunachal Pradesh 65,882 sq km
- Chhattisgarh 55,812 sq km

States with Highest Forest Share of Geographical Area
- Lakshadweep *91.33* percent
- Mizoram *85.34* percent
- Andaman and Nicobar Islands *81.62* percent

Forest Cover Threshold Achievements
- 19 states/UTs have above 33 percent forest cover
- Eight states/UTs above 75 percent forest cover
- Mizoram
- Lakshadweep
- Andaman & Nicobar Island
- Arunachal Pradesh
- Nagaland
- Meghalaya
- Tripura
- Manipur

Carbon Stock
- Total forest carbon stock *7,285.5 million tonnes*
- Increase compared to last assessment 81.5 million tonnes
- Top states by carbon stock:
- *Arunachal Pradesh* 1,021 mt
- Madhya Pradesh 608 mt
- Chhattisgarh 505 mt
- Maharashtra 465 mt
- Total carbon in CO2 equivalent ~30.43 billion tonnes
- Increase since 2005 base year ~2.29 billion tonnes

Regional Highlights
- Western Ghats Eco-Sensitive Areas total 60,285.61 sq km
- 73 percent of WGESA under forest
- North Eastern region forest and tree cover 1,74,394.70 sq km
- 67 percent of geographical area in NE region

Mangrove Cover
- Total mangrove cover *4,991.68* sq km
- *0.15* percent of geographical area
- *Decrease of 7.43* sq km since last assessment
- State changes:
- Gujarat decrease 36.39 sq km
- Andhra Pradesh increase 13.01 sq km
- Maharashtra increase 12.39 sq km

Forest Fire Incidence
- Top states with most fire incidents:
- Uttarakhand
- Odisha
- Chhattisgarh

Policy Commitments
- India NDC under Paris Agreement to create 2.5–3.0 billion tonnes additional carbon sink by 2030
- Bonn Challenge pledge — restore 26 million hectares degraded land by 2030
