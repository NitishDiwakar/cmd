Mesina Strait

Cook Strait (New Zealand)

Taurus Strait

Bass Strait (Australia)

Bering Strait (Alaska-Russia)

Dardanelles Strait (Turkey)

  - Baffin Bay & Atlantic Ocean

Dover strait (England-France)

Florida Strait
  - Gulf of Mexico & Atlantic Ocean
