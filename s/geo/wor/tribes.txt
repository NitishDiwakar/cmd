Title : Tribes and their locations
Tags  : #nomail #noview
Descr :
============================

Aborigines  
  - Queensland, Northern Territory and Western Australia (Australia)  

Afrikaner  
  - South Africa  

Amerinds  
  - North and South America  

Bantus  
  - Central and Southern Africa  

Bedouins  
  - Africa and Southwest Asia (Arabian and Syrian deserts)  

Berbers  
  - Morocco, Algeria and Tunisia  

Bushmen  
  - Kalahari Desert (Botswana, Namibia, Angola, Zambia, Zimbabwe, Lesotho, South Africa)  

Eskimos  
  - Siberia (Russia), Alaska, Canada and Greenland  

Gaucho  
  - Pampas (Uruguay and Argentina)  

Hamites  
  - North-West Africa  

Hottentots  
  - Tropical Africa  

Kaffris  
  - South Africa  

Kirghiz  
  - Asia (Steppe region)  

Magyars  
  - Hungary  

Maoris  
  - New Zealand  

Masai  
  - East Africa  

Masuds  
  - Waziristan (Pakistan)  

Moors  
  - Morocco  

Moros  
  - Philippines (Mindanao and other southern parts)  

Pygmies  
  - Zaire Basin (Africa)  

Red Indians  
  - North America  

Swahili  
  - Kenya and Tanzania  

Tartar  
  - Siberia  

Tenharim  
  - Amazonas (Brazil)  

Yakuts  
  - Tundra region in Russia  

Zulus  
  - South Africa (KwaZulu-Natal)
