Subhas Chandra Bose
- Born *23 January 1897*
- Birthplace Cuttack, Bengal Presidency, British India
- Died 18 August 1945 (reported)
- Death place Taipei, Japanese Taiwan (reported air crash)
- Popular name Netaji
- Indian nationalist leader

Family Background
- Father *Janakinath Bose*, lawyer
- Mother *Prabhavati Devi*
- 14 siblings

Education
- Presidency College, Calcutta
- Scottish Church College, Calcutta
- BA Philosophy
- University of Cambridge
- Indian Civil Service examination qualified 1920
- Resigned from ICS 1921

Political Ideology
- Militant nationalism
- Anti British imperialism
- Believed in armed struggle
- Socialist orientation
- Authoritarian leadership preference

Indian National Congress
- Joined Congress *1921*
- Close associate of C R Das
- *Mayor of Calcutta - 1930*
- President of INC *1938* Haripura Session
- President of INC *1939* Tripuri Session
- Resigned 1939 after conflict with Mahatma Gandhi and Congress leadership

Forward Bloc
- Founded All India Forward Bloc 1939
- Aim to consolidate left wing forces

Imprisonment and Escape
- Frequently imprisoned by British
- House arrest 1940
- Escaped January 1941 from Calcutta
- Travelled to Germany via Afghanistan and Soviet Union

Activities in Germany
- Formed Free India Centre
- Organized Indian Legion from POWs
- Broadcasts from Radio Berlin

Move to Southeast Asia
- Reached Japanese controlled Singapore 1943
- Took leadership of Indian National Army

Indian National Army INA
- Originally formed by Mohan Singh
- Reorganized by Bose
- Objective armed struggle against British
- Slogan Give me blood and I will give you freedom
- Rani of Jhansi Regiment womenâ€™s unit

Provisional Government of Free India
- Formed 21 October 1943
- Named Azad Hind Government
- Declared war against Britain and USA
- Recognized by Axis powers
- Issued currency and stamps

Military Campaign
- INA fought alongside Japanese forces
- Battles of Imphal and Kohima 1944
- Military setback after Japanese defeat

Death Controversy
- Reported death in air crash 18 August 1945
- At Taihoku Airport Taiwan
- Death remains controversial
- Several commissions of inquiry post independence

Major Slogans
- Jai Hind
- Delhi Chalo
- Give me blood and I will give you freedom

Honours and Legacy
- National hero in India
- Birthday 23 January observed as *Parakram Diwas*
- Significant role in radicalizing freedom struggle

Major Differences with Gandhi
- Gandhi supported non violence
- Bose supported armed resistance
- Disagreement over pace and method of independence
