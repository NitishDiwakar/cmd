Title : Dynasties - Medieval India
Tags  : #
Descr : Dynasty, Capital, Founder
============================


Delhi Sultanate
- Period 1206 to 1526
- Capital Delhi

Mamluk Dynasty 1206–1290
- Also called Slave Dynasty

Qutb al-Din Aibak 1206–1210
- Founder of Delhi Sultanate
- Started construction of Qutb Minar

Aram Shah 1210–1211
- Short reign

Shams al-Din Iltutmish 1211–1236
- Consolidated Sultanate
- Completed Qutb Minar
- Introduced silver tanka and copper jital

Rukn al-Din Firuz 1236
- Short reign

Razia Sultan 1236–1240
- First and only female ruler of Delhi Sultanate

Muiz al-Din Bahram 1240–1242
- Weak rule

Ala al-Din Masud 1242–1246
- Deposed by nobles

Nasir al-Din Mahmud 1246–1266
- Real power with Ghiyas al-Din Balban

Ghiyas al-Din Balban 1266–1287
- Strengthened monarchy
- Policy of blood and iron

Muiz al-Din Qaiqabad 1287–1290
- Last Mamluk ruler

Khalji Dynasty 1290–1320

Jalal al-Din Firuz Khalji 1290–1296
- Founded Khalji dynasty

Ala al-Din Khalji 1296–1316
- Market control reforms
- Expansion into Deccan
- Mongol resistance

Qutb al-Din Mubarak Shah 1316–1320
- Reversed some policies of Alauddin

Khusro Khan 1320
- Last Khalji ruler

Tughlaq Dynasty 1320–1414

Ghiyas al-Din Tughlaq 1320–1325
- Founded Tughlaq dynasty
- Built Tughlaqabad

Muhammad bin Tughlaq 1325–1351
- Capital shift to Daulatabad
- Token currency experiment

Firoz Shah Tughlaq 1351–1388
- Public works
- Canals and cities
- Firoz Shah Kotla

Later Tughlaqs 1388–1414
- Decline period

Sayyid Dynasty 1414–1451

Khizr Khan 1414–1421
- Founded Sayyid dynasty

Mubarak Shah 1421–1434

Muhammad Shah 1434–1445

*Alam Shah* 1445–1451
- Last Sayyid ruler

LODI DYNASTY 1451–1526

Bahlul Lodi 1451–1489
- Founded Lodi dynasty

*Sikandar Lodi* 1489–1517
- Founded Agra city

Ibrahim Lodi 1517–1526
- Defeated in First Battle of Panipat
- End of Delhi Sultanate
