*Buddha*
  Born: 563 BC (Lumbini, Nepal)
  Sakya Clan
  Parents: Suddhodana, Mayadevi
  1st teacher: *Alara Kalama*
  Chairoiteer: Channa

*Event & Symbol*
  Avakranti - White elephant
  Jati (birth) - Lotus & Bull
  *Mahabhiniskramana* - Horse (Kanthak)

  Nirvan - Bodhi Tree (Uruvella)
  Dhammachakra parivarthan - Wheel
  Mahaparinirvana - Stupa

*5 Teachings of Buddha*
  - No killing
  - Absentation from theft
  - Absentation from sexual misconduct
  - Absentation from falsehood
  - Absentation from intoxication


*4 Arya Satya* (Noble Truth)
  1. Sabbam dukkham (World is full of misery)
  2. Pratitya Samputpada (Desire is root cause of sorrow)
  3. Dukha nirodha (Desire can be conquered)
  4. Astangika Marga (8-fold path)
    -> Right View
    -> Right Intension
    -> Right Speech
    -> Right Action
    -> Right Livelihood
    -> Right Mindfulness
    -> Right Effort
    -> Right Concentration

Vinaya Pitaka
  - code of conduct

*Sutta Pitaka* (main teachings)
  - Digha Nikaya
  - Majjhima Nikaya
  - Samyutta Nikaya
  - Anguttara Nikaya
  - Khuddaka NIkaya

Buddhist Scholars
  - Mogaliputta Tissa
  - Ashvaghosa
  - Nagarjuna (*Sunyavaad*)
  - Buddhaghosh (Visuddhimarg)
  - Dharmakirti (Teacher at Nalanda, *Kant of India*)


Buddha-protectors
  - Vajrapani
  - Manjusri
  - Avlokiteshwara

  - Theravadin

Paramita
