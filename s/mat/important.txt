1. If the cost price of 16 tables is equal to the selling price of 12 tables, the the gain % is: (ASO Main)
(A) 30
(B) 15
(C) 33x1/3 ✔
(D) 20

  -> 4SP  = 3CP
  -> 1
  -> gain % = 1/3 * 100
  -> 33x1/3

*clocks*
1. When it is 25 past 4 on the clock, the angle between its minute hand and hour hand is: (ASO Mains)
(A) 17.5° ✔
(B) 13.5°
(C) 19°
(D) 10°
  -> *(30H +- 11/2M)*
  -> 30 x 4 +- 11/2 x 25
  -> 120 +- 137.5
  -> 17.5
