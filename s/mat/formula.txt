*Divisibility*

Divisibility of 7
  - double the last digit and subtract it from the remaining number

Divisibility of 11
  - alternately subtract and add its digits from left to right
  - should be 0 or 11

Divisibility of 13
  - Separate the last digit of the number.
  - Multiply this last digit by 4.
  - Add this product to the remaining truncated part of the number.
  - Repeat this process if necessary.
