Author      : Nitish Kumar Diwakar
Date        : 2026-02-13

Description :
==========================================


PSLV-C61/EOS-09 mission launched India’s all-weather radar satellite on 18 May 2025. 

GSLV-F16/NISAR, a joint NASA-ISRO Earth-observation radar satellite, was launched on 30 Jul 2025. 

LVM3-M5/CMS-03 mission successfully placed CMS-03 satellite into orbit on 2 Nov 2025. 

PSLV-C62/EOS-N1 mission on 12 Jan 2026 failed due to third-stage anomaly. 

ISRO identified a safe landing zone for the upcoming Chandrayaan-4 lunar sample return mission. 

Preparations continued for ISRO’s Gaganyaan uncrewed test missions ahead of crewed flights. 

ISRO readied GISAT-1A Earth observation satellite for launch to improve imaging capacity.
