*1-matter-in-our-surroundings*

Kinetic Energy
  - Energy an object has because of its motion

Melting point of ice
  -> 273.15K

Boiling point of water
  -> 373K

*Sublimition* 
  - Solid to gas

*Deposition* 
  - Gas to solid

Atmosphere (atm) 
  - Unit of measuring pressure 

Acetone
  - In nail polish

Bose Einstein Condensate (BEC)
  - 5th state of matter
  - Nobel pr. 2001
  - Cooling a gas of extremely low density
  - Eric A. Cornell; Wolfgang Ketterle; Carl E. Wieman

*Latent heat* of vaporisation 
  - Heat energy required to change 1 kg of liquid to gas at atmospheric pressure at its boiling point

*Quantity-Unity-Symbol*
Temperature
  - Kelvin (K)
Weight
  - Newton (N)
Volume
  - Cubic metre (m^3)
Density
  - kilogram per cubic metre (kg m^3)
